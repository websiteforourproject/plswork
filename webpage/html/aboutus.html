Establishment of the Colegio de Montalban. The Sangguniang Bayan of Rodriguez, Rizal passed Municipal Ordinance No. 03-24, s. 2003 on September 23, 2003, which established the Pamantasan ng Montalban. On July 6, 2004, the Pamantasan ng Montalban was authorized to operate through the Municipal Ordinance No. 04-03. By virtue of Sangguniang Bayan ng Rodriguez Ordinance No. 14-16, Pamantasan ng Montalban was renamed Colegio de Montalban: On October 20, 2014, Sangguniang Panlalawigan ng Rizal Resolution No. 193, Series of 2014, confirmed the renaming of Pamantasan ng Montalban to Colegio de Montalban.
